About The Artist

Welcome to Kiwi Daisy Art Studio, where Jackie Matheney creates light-hearted, fun, and crafty art with a loving touch. With years of scrapbooking experience, she transforms her basement studio into a space filled with creativity and color. Each piece from Kiwi Daisy Art Studio reflects her passion for crafting, offering unique, playful designs that bring joy and warmth to any space. Explore the collection and experience the whimsical charm of handcrafted art made with love and care.
